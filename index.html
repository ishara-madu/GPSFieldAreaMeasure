Privacy Policy

GPS Field Area Measure

GPS Field Area Measure is a free mobile application designed to help users measure land and field areas accurately using GPS technology. This page explains how user data is handled.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect or store personally identifiable information. All measurements are processed locally on your device.

  • Location (GPS): Used only to calculate area measurements while the app is active.
  • No location data is stored or shared by the developer.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information to show personalized or non-personalized ads.

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Approximate location

GPS Field Area Measure does not control how Google collects or uses this data. Learn more here:
